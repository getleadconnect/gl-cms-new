How to Setup CRM Account

Step 1: Getlead account signup

The first step is to sign up on the Getlead CRM webpage. If you already have an account then sign in using a mobile number and password.

Step 2: Add Designation

The first step is to sign up on the Getlead CRM webpage. If you already have an account then sign in using a mobile number and password.

Step 3: Add Departments

Every organization will have various departments and the details of all of it can be added separately in the CRM.

Access Path
Home>Settings>Account settings>Departments
How to add departments
  • 1. The signup process will lead you to the CRM Homepage.
  • 2. Click on the Settings icon provided on the page.
  • 3. Click on the Account settings. This will show you a list of items that fall under this category.
  • 4. Click on Departments.
